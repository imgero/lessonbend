Blueberry Audio Route: Same Amount, New Fraction Name

Listen first: equal whole, equal parts, same amount. Then use equal-sized fraction circles to name, create, and explain equivalent fractions.

whole: One complete circle. · equal parts: Parts of the whole that are the same size. · denominator: The bottom number. It tells how many equal parts make the whole. · numerator: The top number. It tells how many equal parts are selected.
